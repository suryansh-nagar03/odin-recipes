Shahi Paneer


Description:

Shahi Paneer: A royal Indian dish featuring soft paneer cubes immersed in a creamy, cashew-infused tomato sauce, enriched with aromatic spices—a sumptuous delight for the senses.


Ingredients:

  • 250 grams Paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts, soaked in water
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish


Steps:

  1. Prepare Cashew Paste:
    • Grind the soaked cashew nuts into a smooth paste by adding a little water. Set aside.
  2. Sauté Onions and Spices:
    • Heat ghee or oil in a pan. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
    • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw aroma disappears.
    • Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  3. Add Spice Powders:
    • Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, ground coriander, ground cumin, garam masala, and salt to the onion-tomato mixture. Mix well and cook for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add Cashew Paste and Paneer:
    • Pour the cashew paste into the mixture. Stir and cook for a few minutes until the mixture thickens.
    • Add the cubed paneer into the sauce. Mix gently, allowing the paneer to absorb the flavors. Let it simmer for 5-7 minutes on low heat.
  5. Finish with Cream and Sugar:
    • Pour fresh cream into the curry and stir gently. Add sugar if desired, to balance the flavors. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Garnish and Serve:
    • Shahi Paneer is ready! Garnish it with chopped coriander leaves.
    • Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
