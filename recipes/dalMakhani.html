Dal Makhani


Description:

Dal Makhani is a classic Indian dish known for its creamy and velvety texture. It's a flavorful combination of black lentils and kidney beans cooked slowly with aromatic spices, tomatoes, butter, and cream, resulting in a rich and indulgent lentil curry that's immensely satisfying.


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole black lentils (urad dal)
  • 1/4 cup red kidney beans (rajma)
  • 3 cups water (for pressure cooking)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish


Steps:

  1. Prepare Lentils and Kidney Beans:
    • Wash urad dal and rajma together and soak them in water for at least 4-6 hours or overnight. Rinse thoroughly before cooking.
    • In a pressure cooker, add the soaked lentils and kidney beans with fresh water. Pressure cook until they are soft and mushy. Set aside.
  2. Sauté Aromatics:
    • Heat ghee or butter with oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
    • Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chili (if using). Cook until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add Spice Powders:
    • Add tomato puree to the onion mixture. Cook until the oil separates from the sides.
    • Stir in red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for a couple of minutes.
  4. Combine Cooked Lentils and Beans:
    • Add the cooked dal and rajma to the tomato-spice mixture. Mix thoroughly and let it simmer on low heat for about 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Mash some of the lentils with the back of a spoon for a creamier texture.
  5. Finish with Cream and Garnish:
    • Pour in the cream and mix well. Let it simmer for another 5-10 minutes.
    • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  6. Serve and Enjoy:
    • Our creamy and aromatic Dal Makhani is ready to be served! Enjoy it hot with naan, rice, or your choice of bread.
